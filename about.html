in the basements and apartments of canada's capital city dwell four individuals who on occasion convene to pick up their instruments and create a cacophony of beautiful, ugly noise. apostlebird is a band from ottawa, ontario, canada. they consist of vocalist/guitarist hong yue wang, bassist/keyboardist liam digweed, vocalist/guitarist thomas mallo and drummer norman tung. apostlebird has released one single entitled 'twin pandas/lomography'. it was released in june of 2026.
